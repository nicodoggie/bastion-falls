Shadow Construct

Medium construct, neutral evil
Armor Class: 18 (Natural Armor)
Hit Points: 45 (6d10 + 12)
Speed: 30 ft.

STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA
16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 5 (-3) | 10 (+0) | 5 (-3)

Damage Resistances: Cold, Necrotic; Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks
Condition Immunities: Charmed, Exhaustion, Frightened, Paralyzed, Poisoned
Senses: Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 10
Languages: Understands the commands of its creator
Challenge: 3 (700 XP)

Features:

    Shadow Glide: Can move through dim light or darkness without provoking opportunity attacks.

Actions:

    Multiattack: The Construct makes two slam attacks.
    Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
        Hit: 11 (2d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage.

Reactions:

    Shadow Absorption: When hit with radiant damage, the construct absorbs part of the light, reducing damage by 10 and gaining temporary hit points equal to the reduced damage.
